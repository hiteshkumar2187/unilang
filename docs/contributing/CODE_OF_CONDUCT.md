# Code of Conduct

UniLang follows the [Apache Software Foundation Code of Conduct](https://www.apache.org/foundation/policies/conduct.html).

## Summary

- **Be respectful and inclusive.** We welcome contributions from everyone regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.
- **Be collaborative.** Work together to resolve conflicts constructively.
- **Be professional.** Harassment and exclusionary behavior are not acceptable.
- **Be considerate.** Your work will be used by other people, and you in turn will depend on the work of others.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to conduct@unilang.apache.org. All reports will be handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from the project at the discretion of the maintainer team.
